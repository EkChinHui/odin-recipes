Sprite

Description

This is a homemade Sprite recipe I made in my spare time one day. It tastes just like the real thing but without the extra chemicals.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons white sugar, or more to taste
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice, or more to taste
  • 16 ounces lime-flavoured seltzer water

Steps

  1. Combine sugar and lemon juice in a glass. Slowly pour in seltzer water and stir until well combined.
